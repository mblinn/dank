The Daniel M. Kenny Jr. Memorial Scholarship Fund

The fund was organized to honor the memory of Daniel M. Kenny, Jr., who passed away on August 23, 2007 at the age of 55. Through this memorial, a scholarship fund will be established for deserving graduating seniors of Carmel High School, located in Carmel, New York. In addition, the fund plans to sponsor public awareness programs relating to physical fitness and heart-related health issues.
